Require Check-out

When enabled, members must check out before they can check in again. This helps track visit duration and facility capacity.

Auto Check-out

Automatically checks out members who haven't manually checked out after the specified time period. Prevents overnight check-ins.

Multiple Check-ins

Allows members to check in multiple times for different services without checking out first. Useful for facilities with multiple areas.

Service Type Requirement

Forces staff to specify what service the member is using. Helpful for usage analytics and capacity management.
