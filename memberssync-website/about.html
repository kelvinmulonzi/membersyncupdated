About MemberSync

The Smart Way to Manage Members

MemberSync is a cloud-based membership management software developed by Next Solution. Designed specifically for the African market, it understands the realities of organizations across Cameroon, Central Africa, and the continent.

We saw churches maintaining paper attendance books. Businesses running loyalty programs on handwritten cards. Schools tracking fees in physical notebooks. Gyms with no idea who had paid for the month. MemberSync was built to solve all of that.

Today, MemberSync serves organizations of all sizes ‚Äî from small community groups to multi-branch businesses ‚Äî giving them a professional, secure, and scalable platform at an accessible price.
